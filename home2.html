The purpose of The GeoEffect is to enlighten and spread awareness regarding climate change and its impact on Earth. Through this website we hope to inform you of ways one can contribute to the eradication of this harsh reality our planet is facing. Every individual can make a difference and collectively we can restore our planet to great health; however, this is only possible when we all do our part. Climate change is real and there is limited time left to save the planet. Think Globally, Act Locally !
